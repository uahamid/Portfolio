Combining creativity with a keen understanding of brand identity, I ensured that each graphic adhered to both the association's and UC Davis's established visual guidelines. Through the strategic use of color, typography, and imagery, I crafted compelling visuals that effectively communicated the benefits and value propositions of each association.
